Objection Handlers

"But ServiceNow is the industry standard..."

Response: "ServiceNow is indeed the standard for enterprises with 1,000+ employees and dedicated ITSM teams. But for organizations your size, it's dramatically overengineered. Companies in your segment report saving 70-80% on licensing costs alone when switching from ServiceNow, plus eliminating consultant fees. You get enterprise-grade ITSM without the enterprise complexity and cost that doesn't fit your business model."

"We're already invested in Atlassian/JIRA..."

Response: "That makes sense if you're primarily focused on Dev-IT collaboration. However, consider this: JIRA was built for software development and adapted for service management, while Zendesk was purpose-built for service delivery. Plus, Zendesk's AI capabilities are included rather than charged per conversation like JIRA's $0.30+ consumption model. And our omnichannel support—phone, chat, social, SMS—is native, not an add-on. Are your employees only submitting tickets through a portal, or do they need to reach IT multiple ways?"

"Freshservice is cheaper at the entry level..."

Response: "You're right that Freshservice's starter tier is lower. But consider what you're comparing: Freshservice has 20,000 customers globally, while Zendesk has 165,000+—that's 8x more proven deployments. Freshservice users consistently cite reporting limitations, integration challenges, and support inconsistency. When you factor in the full picture—superior omnichannel, deeper analytics, stronger AI, and proven scale—Zendesk delivers better ROI. Plus, our platform serves customer AND employee service, giving you optionality as you grow."

"We're looking at Halo because everything's included..."

Response: "Halo is a solid UK-based player with an all-inclusive model. However, consider: Zendesk has 1,500+ integrations vs. their 200+, giving you 7x more flexibility to connect with your existing tools. We have 165,000+ customers vs. their 100,000 total users, meaning deeper community support and proven scale. And critically, if you ever need to leverage your investment for customer service—whether that's customer success, support, or CX—Zendesk gives you that optionality. Halo locks you into pure ITSM."

"Zendesk is known for customer service, not ITSM..."

Response: "That's actually our strategic advantage. Think about it: the best employee experiences mirror the best customer experiences—intuitive, omnichannel, AI-powered. Our customer service DNA means we've perfected the user experience that drives adoption. Plus, many IT leaders are discovering that employee service issues and customer impact are connected—when employees can't work, customers suffer. Zendesk gives you one unified view. Our ITSM capabilities are enterprise-grade; we're just not enterprise-complicated."
